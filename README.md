# Dynamic Sales & Customer Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview
This project features interactive Tableau dashboards designed to help sales leaders and executives analyze sales performance and customer behavior. The dashboards support year-over-year comparisons, trend analysis, product profitability, and customer insights.

## Tools Used
- Tableau Desktop
- Tableau Public

## Dashboards

### Sales Dashboard
- KPI overview: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity (YoY comparison)
- Monthly trends with highest and lowest months highlighted
- Sales and profit by product subcategory
- Weekly sales and profit compared against the average
- Interactive filters by year, product, and location

![Sales Dashboard](Sales%20Dashboard%20.png)

---

### Customer Dashboard
- KPI overview: Total Customers, Sales per Customer, Total Orders (YoY)
- Monthly customer trends
- Customer distribution by number of orders
- Top 10 customers by profit with last order date aligned to selected year
- Interactive filters by year, product, and location

![Customer Dashboard](Customer%20Dashboard.png)
